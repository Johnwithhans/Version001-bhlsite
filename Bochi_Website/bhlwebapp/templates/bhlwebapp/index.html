About Bochi Hospital Limited

Establishemt: Bochi Hospital Limited is a distinguished privately-owned medical institution dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services to the general populace. Originally established as a health center in 2011, the hospital underwent significant expansion, meeting stringent criteria set by the Ministry of Health, which led to its upgrade to a fully-fledged hospital in 2015. It received certification (No. 071460, dated 28/1/2015) and was accredited as a Level 2 Specialized Hospital in November 2015.

Situated at Plot No. 1019, Block 'B,' MbeziTemboni area, Kinondoni municipality, Dar es Salaam, Bochi Hospital boasts over 160 beds, including private rooms. Accessible via an unnamed gravel road branching off from Morogoro Road at the "KwaMsuguri" commuter bus stand, it is a beacon of healthcare excellence.

Vision: To emerge as a leader in the healthcare industry.

Mission Statement: Our mission at Bochi Hospital is to ensure that every individual stepping through our doors feels genuinely cared for, valued, and respected.

Passion: Passion serves as the driving force that propels our organizational culture.

Credit Facility: Since its inception in 2011, Bochi Hospital has been committed to providing both credit and cash-paid services. Notably, credit facilities have been extended to esteemed companies with ongoing service agreements. Current partnerships include Puma Company, Resolution Health East Africa Limited, Strategies Insurance (Tanzania) Limited, Tanzania Electrical Supply Company, Jubilee Insurance Company, and PPF Pension Fund.

Management: Bochi Hospital operates as a privately owned limited company, overseen by a dedicated Board of Directors who serve as policy-makers. Day-to-day management is executed by a proficient team, including the Managing Director, Director, and other key personnel such as Doctors in Charge, Matron, Senior Doctors, Accountant, and Human Resource Manager. Regular board meetings, held monthly, facilitate the presentation of performance and management reports by the Managing Director. These reports guide strategic decisions and ensure the hospital's continued commitment to excellence.
